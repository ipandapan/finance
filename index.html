📐 Methodology · 计算方法
Monthly rate = annual rate ÷ 12. Contributions are applied at month-end (balance × 1 + monthly rate) then new contribution added). From month 13 onward, annual step-up increases the monthly contribution by the step-up rate each year: contribution(m) = C × (1 + g)⌊(m−1)/12⌋.
Real purchasing power discounts the final balance by cumulative inflation: FV / (1 + inflation)t.
月利率 = 年利率 ÷ 12。每月末定投（先计息再加投）。第 13 个月起每年按递增率调整月供。实际购买力按通胀复利折现。

📐 Formula · 公式
Equal monthly installment (amortized): M = P × r × (1+r)n / ((1+r)n − 1)
When r = 0: M = P / n. Monthly payment is rounded to cents; the final period absorbs any rounding residual so remaining principal reaches exactly zero.
等额本息还款。月供精确到分，最后一期调整差额使剩余本金归零。利率为 0 时月供 = 本金 ÷ 期数。
